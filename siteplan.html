Site Name

Site Name: Lagos River Cruise & Events

Description: This name is a professional expansion of the acronym (LRC&E). It clearly identifies the location (Lagos), the core business (River Cruise), and the secondary offering (Events), covering all aspects of the business. It is professional and easily searchable.

Domain Name: lagosrivercruise.com

Site Purpose

The Lagos River Cruise website will be the premier online destination for booking unique dining and event experiences on the first floating boat cruise restaurant in West Africa. The site's scope is to:

  • Provide an **extensive, detailed overview** of all dining packages (breakfast, lunch, and dinner cruises).
  • Facilitate **secure ticket purchasing** for scheduled cruises.
  • Manage **reservation applications** for private venue hire and corporate events.
  • Establish the brand as a luxurious, unique, and safe entertainment destination in Lagos.

Scenarios (Target Audience Questions)

These questions drive the content and functionality of the site.

  1. **"What are the available times and prices for the weekend dinner cruise, and can I book tickets for a party of six people right now?"** (Drives: Pricing tables, Scheduling/Calendar, and the primary Reservation System functionality)
  2. **"I'm planning a corporate retreat. What is the capacity of the vessel, and how do I submit a request to hire the entire boat for a private event?"** (Drives: Services page detail on vessel capacity, and a dedicated Hire/Event Application form)
